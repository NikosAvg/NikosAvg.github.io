About Me

Hello! I’m a dedicated Mathematician and Computer Scientist, deeply passionate about education and the application of technology to solve pressing environmental and societal challenges. Currently, I am a Mathematics Teacher at ALFA EKAPAIDEUTIKI, where I engage in shaping young minds and fostering a love for mathematics. Alongside my teaching role, I’ve been actively involved in various research and development projects aimed at addressing environmental issues and enhancing disaster management systems.
